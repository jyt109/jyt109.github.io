Geometric Mean:

When to use: 1. Percentage of growth on a yearly basis, e.g. Year 1: 1%, Year 2: 2%, etc 2. Comparing two metrics on different scales, number of likes and number of comments 3. Average of ratios for different time points of a metric, e.g. Female : Male at different time points 4. Usually used in time series data 5. Using Arithmetic Mean for percentage growth is likely to overestimate the effect

Cavet 1. All values must be larger than zero

Formula:

\(\sqrt[n]{(x_1)(x_2)(x_1)\ldots}\)
